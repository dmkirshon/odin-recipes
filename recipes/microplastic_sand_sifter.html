Microplastic Sand Sifter

Main Page

Description

This DIY project is for a microplastic sand sifter. The sifter will filter out all those annoying, tiny and lethal plastics at your next beach cleanup. Guess what gary the seagull, you have someone watching your back. Let's not even get started on nurdles! Go do good work! This DIY is thanks to the Kailua Beach Adventures: Microplastics Sand Sifter.

Ingredients

  • Duct/Masking/Painters Tape
  • 1/8" Wire Mesh
  • Wire Sheers or Scissors

Steps

  1. Cut wire mesh to desired size, recommended around 20"x15".
  2. Trim wire mesh to keep rectangle as even and straight as possible.
  3. Add 1 layer of tape to the sides of the wire mesh and fold over mesh so a protective barrier is formed on all sides of the mesh.
  4. Add any additonal tape as needed for safety and comfort.
  5. Go save the world!
