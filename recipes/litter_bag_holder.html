DIY Litter Bag Holder

Main Page

Description

This DIY is for a litter bag holder to assist in picking up litter/rubbish/garbage. The holder will keep the bag open to allow easy accessibility for items to be placed in the bag. This DIY is thanks to Thriftyfun: Litter Bag Holder.

Ingredients

  • 36" X 1/4 inch aluminum rod
  • 37" X 1/4 inch vinyl tubing
  • 4 mini size heavy-duty metal spring clamps

Steps

  1. Place the aluminum rod within the vinyl tubing
  2. Using a vice, bend the rod in the middle.
  3. 4" down from the bent portion bend again on both sides of the rod to form a slightly larger than 90 degree angle.
  4. 7" down from the last bent portion, on both sides, bend again so the last section of the rod is parallel to the initial middle bend.
  5. Wrap half the opening of a bag over the rod U shape and clamp on each straight edge with the clamps
  6. Save the world!
