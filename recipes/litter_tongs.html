DIY Litter Tongs

Main Page

Description

These wooden litter tongs are the bee's knees. They are made from 100% recyclable material; where most litter grabbers are made from plastics. They will help you pick up any far reaching rubbish/trash/garbage while saving your back, and any other angsty body parts, from turmoil. Follow the steps below to make this amazing gadget and help save the earth, and donald the dolphin, today! All credit goes to Instructables: DIY Litter Tongs.

Ingredients

  • 1 - 8'x1"x.25" timber
  • 4 - 1.5" wood screws
  • Power Drill
  • 4 - Clamps

Steps

  1. Cut the wood into two pieces measuring 46 inches, cut the scrap wood into 6 - 2" pieces.
  2. Fit two clamps, one near the center and one near the tong's jaws, while making sure the two wooden pieces are aligned.
  3. Place 2 of the 2" scraps at the top, in between the two main wood pieces, and the remaining 4 pieces 5.5" to 6.5" below the top. Make sure to confirm alignment of all wooden pieces and clamp all them together. The ending result should be .5" to a 1" gap transition between the two main pieces with about 4.5" between the scrap blocks.
  4. Pre-drill holes through both sides of the main pieces that are centered at the scrap blocks.
  5. Screw the 4 screws into each pre-drilled hole
  6. Save the world!
